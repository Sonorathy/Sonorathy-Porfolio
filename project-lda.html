Legion D'Antares

Luxury fashion ecommerce — custom storefront design & development.

Year2026
ScopeWeb Design & Development
ClientLegion D'Antares
Duration5 weeks

The Scenario & Business Problem

The brand needed an ecommerce presence that matched its high-fashion positioning instead of a generic template look. I designed and built a custom storefront with editorial pacing that lets the collections breathe.

Off-the-shelf ecommerce themes made every collection look like a catalog page. The brand needed the site itself to feel like part of the campaign, not just a place to check out.

The Design & Strategy Challenge

The goal was a storefront that felt as considered as the collections it sells, without sacrificing conversion. Core objectives:

  • Design an Editorial Storefront: Give each collection room to breathe instead of a dense grid layout.
  • Tell the Product Story: Pair imagery and copy so product pages read like a lookbook.
  • Simplify Checkout: Cut friction from cart to confirmation without losing the premium feel.
