Park View

Boutique hotel brand identity — visual language & art direction.

Year2026
ScopeBrand & Art Direction
ClientPark View
Duration3 weeks

The Scenario & Business Problem

The hotel's existing identity felt generic and didn't reflect its boutique character. I developed a distinctive visual language — from logotype to photography direction — that positions it as a premium, memorable stay.

Leadership wanted a brand that could carry the same weight across a website, print collateral, and in-room signage, without diluting the boutique feel that sets the property apart.

The Design & Strategy Challenge

The goal was a visual identity distinctive enough to stand out in a crowded hospitality market, while staying flexible across every touchpoint. Core objectives:

  • Define a Distinctive Mark: Design a logotype and visual system that reads as premium, not generic.
  • Direct the Photography: Set the art direction so every shot reinforces the same brand feeling.
  • Document the System: Ship clear guidelines so the identity stays consistent across print and digital.
