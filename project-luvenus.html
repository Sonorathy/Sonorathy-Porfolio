30S Care

Premium leather care brand identity & landing page — clean, care, protect.

Year2026
ScopeBrand Identity & UI/UX Design
Client30S Care
Duration4 weeks

The Scenario & Business Problem

30S Care is a premium leather care line for car interiors entering a market crowded with generic, low-trust accessory brands. It needed an identity and a launch page that could earn confidence in the first few seconds — before a single feature was explained.

The name comes from the product's core promise: visible results in just 30 seconds. The brand and the landing page both had to make that promise feel credible, not gimmicky.

The Design & Strategy Challenge

The goal was a premium, trustworthy identity that could extend cleanly from product packaging to a conversion-focused digital storefront. Core objectives:

  • Build the Brand Identity: Design a logo, color system, and typography that read as premium automotive care, not generic cleaning supplies.
  • Design the Landing Page: Lead with the "30 seconds" promise and guide visitors straight to purchase.
  • Ship a Mini Design System: Document reusable UI components so the product site stays consistent as it grows.
  • Extend to Merchandise: Carry the identity onto apparel and stationery for market presence beyond the product itself.

Deliverables
