M Village

Hotel booking platform — end‑to‑end web app design.

Year2026
ScopeProduct & UI/UX Design
ClientM Village
Duration4 weeks

The Scenario & Business Problem

Guests were dropping off mid-booking because of a cluttered, multi-step flow with unclear pricing and availability. I simplified the journey into a clear, guided flow so guests can find, compare, and book rooms with confidence.

The team needed a booking experience that matched the quality of the stays themselves — effortless from search to confirmation, without adding engineering overhead to maintain.

The Design & Strategy Challenge

The goal was to turn a confusing multi-step process into a guided journey guests could complete in minutes, without hiding the details that build trust. Core objectives:

  • Redesign the Booking Flow: Turn search, compare, and checkout into one clear, guided journey.
  • Build for Reuse: Ship a reusable component library so new pages stay consistent as the product grows.
  • Design for Every Screen: Deliver fully responsive web and mobile views without compromise.
