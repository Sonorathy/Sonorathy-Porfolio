FinB&B

Fintech web app — onboarding, dashboard, and reporting UI.

Year2026
ScopeProduct & UI/UX Design
ClientFinB&B
Duration5 weeks

The Scenario & Business Problem

Early users found the product hard to trust and navigate, with onboarding that felt like a typical finance app. I designed a calmer, clearer interface that builds trust from the first screen.

In fintech, hesitation at onboarding means lost users. The product needed to feel credible and easy to understand before it could ask anyone to connect their finances.

The Design & Strategy Challenge

The goal was an interface that felt trustworthy without feeling cold or overly corporate. Core objectives:

  • Simplify Onboarding: Cut unnecessary steps so new users reach value faster.
  • Design the Dashboard: Make reporting and account data easy to scan at a glance.
  • Build Trust Visually: Use an accessible, calm visual language instead of typical finance-app tropes.
